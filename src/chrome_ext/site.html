Youtube™ Video Preview helps you decide whether it is worth watching a video before clicking it.



It works using:

  1. http://www.youtube.com/ (non secure protocol)
  2. https://www.youtube.com/ (secure protocol)

Features:

  • Mouseover a thumbnail at youtube.com and you will see other thumbnails from that video, by this you can determine if the content is what you are interested in.
  • Below each video you can see rating statistics, this will help you to check if the video is worth watching.
  • Works on Google Chrome minimum version 18.
  • Option to change image rotating time and option to enable/disable rating preview.
  • Option to hide icon from omnibox.
